Privacy Policy

At Ortiz Design & Development, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website ortizdesignanddevelopment.com or engage with our services. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect:

  1. We may collect the following types of information:
  2. Personal Information: When you contact us, request a quote, or engage our services, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any other details you provide.
  3. Non-Personal Information: We may collect data such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage patterns through cookies and similar technologies.
  4. Project-Related Information: If you are a client, we may collect information related to your project, such as design preferences, content, or files you provide to us for website development.

How We Collect Information:

  1. We collect information in the following ways:
  2. Directly from You: When you fill out forms, send emails, or communicate with us.
  3. Automatically: Through cookies, analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics), and server logs as you interact with our website.
  4. From Third Parties: If you contact us via a third-party platform (e.g., social media), we may receive information from those services.

How We Use Your Information:

  1. We use your information to:
  2. Provide, manage, and improve our website design and development services.
  3. Respond to inquiries, provide quotes, and communicate with you about your project.
  4. Process payments and fulfill contractual obligations.
  5. Analyze website usage to enhance user experience and optimize our site.
  6. Send marketing emails or updates (with your consent, where required).
  7. Comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

How We Share Your Information:

  1. We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:
  2. Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., hosting providers, payment processors) who assist us in delivering our services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  3. Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process (e.g., court order).
  4. Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

  1. We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, track usage, and analyze trends. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Essential cookies required for website functionality cannot be disabled.

Data Security:

  1. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no online transmission or storage method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices:

  1. Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
  2. Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  3. Opt out of marketing communications.
  4. Disable cookies (except essential ones) via your browser.
  5. To exercise these rights, please contact us at ortizdesignanddevelopment @gmail.com.

Third-Party Links:

  1. Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy:

  1. Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn such data has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

International Data Transfers:

  1. If you are located outside the United States, be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where our servers are located. We will take steps to ensure your data is handled in compliance with applicable laws.

Extra:

  1. We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Updated Date.” We encourage you to review it periodically.
  2. If you have any questions or concerns regarding these Terms, please contact us at ortizdesignanddevelopment @gmail.com.
