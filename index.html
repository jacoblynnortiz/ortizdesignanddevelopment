About Us

Our Mission

At Ortiz Design & Development, our mission is to bring your business into the 21st century and connect your customers to you online. We are driven by a strong commitment to making you a custom and beautiful website that stands apart from the crowd. We would love to build you a website at an affordable price. With a clear vision for the future, we strive to get as many people as possible a website for their business/organization.

Our Story

At Ortiz Design & Development, our story began with a passion and a vision. It was founded May 3, 2023 by Jacob Ortiz with a clear purpose in mind: to bring your business to the 21st century and connect your customers to you from the web. The journey started back in the summer of 2020 when Jacob decided to start web design and development, he started making websites at just 15 years, and slowly learned the skills required to start making websites for people which he would later do with having just 2 years of experience.
